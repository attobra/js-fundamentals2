Become
who
you are.

You were made in the image of God, and yet you’ve never spent a day of your life fully reflecting that image. Jesus, the only true image bearer, made a way for you to become who you are. Join us as we follow Jesus together.

In a city full of transience and change, we value regular participation in Christian worship because it reminds us of who we are and forms us into who we were made to be.

We gather every Sunday at 10am and 5pm.

We know that sometimes life prevents us from meeting together. For times like these, we offer church online. Join us this week!
